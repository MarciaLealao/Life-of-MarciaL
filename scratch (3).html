My Passion for Astronomy

I got my first telescope when I was twelve. My dad and I set it up in the backyard on a cold October night, pointed it at the moon, and I completely lost it when I could actually see the craters. That was it for me. Now I spend way too much time reading about black holes and watching NASA livestreams at 2am.
