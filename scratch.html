I Love a Good Deal!

Couponing is my favorite way to turn everyday shopping into a game of strategy and savings.

How I Approach Couponing

My process mixes planning, timing, and a little bit of math. Here’s how I break it down:

  1. Getting Started
    • Pick one main grocery store to focus on.
    • Download that store’s app and enable digital coupons.
    • Start with just a few items you buy every week.
  2. Leveling Up
    • Stack store sales with manufacturer coupons.
    • Use cashback apps to double-dip on savings.
    • Track prices so you know when a deal is truly good.
  3. Going Pro
    • Build a small stockpile of non-perishables.
    • Plan meals around what’s on sale.
    • Share extra deals with friends and family.
